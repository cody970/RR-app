## High-Risk Feature Appearances (50+ Tracks)

These are the most likely sources of uncollected royalties. Featured artists frequently miss royalty collection because:
1. The lead artist's label registers the track but not the featured artist's publishing
2. SoundExchange payments require separate registration as a Featured Artist
3. Mechanical royalties at The MLC may not be matched to the featured artist's compositions
