| **No ISWC linkage** | Even when BMI has ISWC, MLC can't match without proper recording-to-work linkage | Systemic |
| **4 different distributors** | Each distributor submitted different metadata to DSPs | All tracks |

### The Matching Failure Chain

```
DSP (Spotify/Apple) pays mechanical royalty to MLC
    ↓
MLC receives payment with ISRC + recording metadata
    ↓
MLC tries to match ISRC to a registered musical work
    ↓
FAILURE POINT 1: No work registered with matching writer name
    → "SADA BABY" ≠ "SORRELL CASADA AARON"
    ↓
FAILURE POINT 2: No ISRC-to-ISWC linkage exists
    → TCADI1706268 not linked to T9321722525
    ↓
FAILURE POINT 3: No work registered at all
    → MILES DEROZAN, OUTSIDE, BAPTISM have no BMI registration
    ↓
RESULT: Mechanical royalties go to UNMATCHED POOL
    ↓
After 3 years → redistributed to major publishers by market share
```
