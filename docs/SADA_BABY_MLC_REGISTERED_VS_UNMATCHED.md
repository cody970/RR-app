## Critical Findings

### 1. 81 BMI Works Missing from MLC Registration

**81 works (23% of BMI catalog) are registered for performance royalties but NOT for mechanical royalties.**

These are **definite mechanical royalty gaps** — the songs are streaming, performance royalties are being collected by BMI, but mechanical royalties are sitting in the MLC unmatched pool (or being redistributed).

**Impact:** Assuming average mechanical accumulation of $5K-$25K per work:
- Conservative: $405K recovery potential
- Aggressive: $2.025M recovery potential

### 2. 74 MLC Works Missing from BMI Registration

**74 works (31% of MLC catalog) are registered for mechanical royalties but NOT for performance royalties.**

These are **definite performance royalty gaps** — the songs are streaming, mechanical royalties are being collected by MLC, but performance royalties are NOT being collected by BMI.

**Impact:** Assuming average performance accumulation of $1K-$10K per work:
- Conservative: $74K recovery potential
- Aggressive: $740K recovery potential

### 3. 27 Triple Gap Works

**27 works are NOT in BMI, NOT in SoundExchange, AND NOT matched in MLC.**

These are the **worst-case scenarios** — zero royalties collected across all platforms.

**Example:** OUTSIDE (FEAT. TRAP MANNY) with ISRC US39N2000249 (Atlantic/Asylum distribution)

### 4. ISWC Coverage Crisis

**Only 31.7% of MLC registered works have ISWCs.**

This is dangerously low. Without ISWCs:
- Recordings cannot be matched to works reliably
- Matching relies on title/artist string matching (less accurate)
- Higher risk of unmatched mechanicals

### 5. High-Value Works Registered but with Issues

- **PRESSIN** and **AKTIVATED** are registered in MLC but have NO ISWC
- **WHOLE LOTTA CHOPPAS REMIX** has ISWC T3076542462 and is registered, but the BMI entry shows:
  - 155% writer over-claim
  - Pending society review since April 2021
  - Nicki Minaj PRO listed as "NA" instead of BMI

The MLC registration may have the same conflicts, causing payment delays.
